Observership with Dr. Carmen Fong, Colorectal Surgery

I shadowed Dr. Carmen Fong at Mount Sinai Beth Israel Hospital in both operating rooms and consultations. In operating rooms, I was exposed to procedures involving hemorrhoids, fissures, colon cancer, diverticulitis, and a robot-assisted surgery.

Dr. Fong would always take the time to thoroughly teach me the complications of each procedure. When we were not making rounds at the hospital or in the OR, Dr. Fong would share her publications and other research articles with me, one being the importance of women empowerment in surgeries, since many women still shy away from surgery as a career path.
